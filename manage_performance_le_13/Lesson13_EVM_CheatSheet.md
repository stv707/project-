# Earned Value Management (EVM) – 1‑Page Cheat Sheet
**Project+ (PK0‑005)**

---

## The Only Question EVM Answers
> Are we getting the value we expected for the time and money spent?

---

## Core Inputs (Given in Exam)

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| PV | Planned Value – work planned |
| EV | Earned Value – work completed |
| AC | Actual Cost – money spent |

---

## Variance (Direction Matters)

| Metric | Formula | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| CV | EV − AC | Positive = under budget |
| SV | EV − PV | Positive = ahead |

---

## Performance Index

| Metric | Formula | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| CPI | EV ÷ AC | >1 good |
| SPI | EV ÷ PV | >1 ahead |

---

## Forecasting

| Metric | Meaning |
|---|---|
| EAC | Expected total cost |
| VAC | Budget over/under |

---

## Exam Rules
- Positive variance is good
- Index above 1 is good
- Meaning > math
